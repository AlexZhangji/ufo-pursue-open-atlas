### E. Highlight slide decks
- western_us_event_slides_5.08.2026.pdf — **The "Western US Event" — the strongest content of the entire release**
  - Federal LE special agents (USPER1-USPER6, three teams of two), Western US, dusk
  - "Orbs Launching Orbs": orange "mother" orbs emit/launch smaller red orbs in groups of 2-4 (3 most common), happened 5+ times across 2 days. Each orange orb visible 1-2 sec, then disappear. Red orbs move horizontally, sometimes "swoop down" or "head up at angle"
  - "Large Fiery Orb": ~12-18m diameter, ~1050m away (per AARO), perched near rock pinnacle, "Eye of Sauron without pupil" or "orange Storm Electrify bowling ball", ~1 minute hover with zero motion
  - "Dark Kite": initially thought a car, then moved laterally without changing orientation, "split second visible in NVGs before light turned off", thin line ~4 ft wide, lifted but stayed flat horizontal — agents lost sight after a few seconds
  - All described by federal LE with NVG, AARO did follow-up site measurements — not random civilian sightings

## Quality assessment

**The richest substantive content is the "Western US Event" slide deck** — federal LE eyewitnesses (multiple teams, NVG, AARO follow-up). The "orbs launching orbs" description is genuinely unusual.

The PR-series (military IR videos) are mostly "single area of contrast, sensor tracks it, then loses it" — typical AARO unresolved cases that could be drones, atmospheric, balloons, sensor artifacts, or genuinely unknown. PR-046 (football-shape three radial projections) and PR-038 (eight-pointed star with trail) are the two most physically distinctive descriptions but still sensor-frame data.

The MR documents (mission reports / range foulers) are the textual companions to the PR videos — pilots' written debriefs. Largely redacted (1.4(a) NSI codes), but contain coordinate / altitude / speed metadata.

The FBI files (190+ page scanned PDFs) appear to be raw archival case files. Without OCR, hard to tell substance, but historically FBI UFO files have been mostly tip lines and witness letters, not government investigations.

The 1948 historical document and Apollo 17 image are "transparency theatre" — already public domain, low-info value.

**No smoking gun for NHI**. AARO's framing on every PR/MR file: *"Readers should not interpret any part of this description as reflecting an analytical judgment, investigative conclusion, or factual determination regarding the described event's validity, nature, or significance."*

**Overall quality**: more substantive than I expected (Western US Event is genuinely interesting), but still no instrument-grade evidence (radar+IR+visual cross-confirmation, recovered material, etc.). Government posture: "we have a lot of unresolved data, you analyze it".
