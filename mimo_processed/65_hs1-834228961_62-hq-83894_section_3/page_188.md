## Office Memorandum • UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

### STANDARD FORM NO. 64

**TO:** MR. LADD
**DATE:** October 9, 1947

**SUBJECT:** FLYING DISCS

There are attached hereto, photographs of various reports received by the Air Corps in connection with the above-captioned matter. These photographs were made available to Special Agent S. W. Reynolds of the Liaison Section by Lieutenant Colonel K. L. Garrett of the Intelligence Division of the Air Force for completion of Bureau files.

**RECOMMENDATION:** It is recommended that this memorandum be made available to Supervisor R. G. Fletcher of the Internal Security Section for his information.

SWR:mm
