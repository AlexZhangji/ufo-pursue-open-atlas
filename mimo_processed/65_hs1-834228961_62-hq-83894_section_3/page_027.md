**UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT**

**Office Memorandum**
**DATE:** 4-25-54

**TO:** Director, FBI
FROM: CIN, Dallas
SUBJECT: NAFI - FLYING SAUCERS
PRIORITY: ( )

REFERENCE is made to the Bureau letter dated July 18, 1947.

The reference letter to the Bureau, no official comment, has advised that a scientific investigation has been completed of the metal particles referred to. They were determined to be of ordinary sheet metal which had been subjected to a very high degree of heat. The heat caused bubbles to be formed on one face iron which were originally thought to be of some metallic alloy.

The scientists examining the particles concluded that if they had come through the air from any great altitude in as small pieces as they were found then most of the heat would have been taken from them by the time they reached the ground and they would not have melted. It is noted they attained approximately 700 feet from a vertical branch and lighter were conducted by FBI to determine whether or not the particles could have been originally a part of a flare in a smoke screen or some other part of a steam engine. These inquiries revealed in positive information that the particles did not come from a bldg. or locomotive. Measurements of four pieces examined revealed that they had most likely been originally all part of one hollow cylinder, slightly larger in diameter and three sixth of an inch in thickness. It is felt that one piece falling from a great height would have still retained a good part of its heat and probably would have smashed when it hit the ground.

A scientist, who saw Burner did not identity by name that coat even given employees of similar measurements had been used in flare rockets researcher work on a guided missile project. However, this individual did not so conclude to the exclusion of all other possibilities.

It is interesting to note that the examination at FBI was actually conducted by the Burner Office which furnished an internal report similar in all major details to that supplied by Burner above. The man at FBI searching through files turned up all additional pieces of the original particles available. These will be turned over to the Brown Outlet in further examination being conducted by FBI with no effort on their part to reconstruct the original evidence...
