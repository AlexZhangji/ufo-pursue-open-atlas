### [Handwritten Account - Main Body]
A close up view of the Flying Saucer as it passed by the Auger Falls Trout Farm at 1:00 pm Aug. 13 going down Snake River Canyon at a height of about 75 ft. from canyon floor at a speed of about 1000 per.

I had a side view at a distance of about 300 ft. and almost on a level, and two of my boys was below me, and saw it at about a 45 degree angle. They got both a bottom and side view. We were all looking at it from the south side looking toward the north. The boys saw it coming about a 1/2 mi. up canyon. We all lost sight of it in less than a mile.

It was all one color sort of a light sky blue with a red tublar fiery glow on the sides of hood.

The canyon floor is rough along here and it rode up and down over the hills and hollows.

It is my opinion that it is guided by instruments and must be powered by atomic power as it made very little noise just a swish as as it passed by.
