## Off-Memorandum

**UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT**

**TO:** Director, FBI
**FROM:** SAC, San Francisco
**DATE:** September 4, 1952
**SUBJECT:** REQUEST FOR FLYING DISCS
**REMARKS:** A - 2, HAMILTON FIELD, CALIF.

Photostatic copy of a letter dated August 28, 1947, from Lt. Col. DONALD L. SUGHRUE, A-2, Hamilton Field, Calif., with an attachment referring to the sighting of a flying object by Captain JAKOB FINK and his co-pilot, VINTON COOK, near Beale AFB.

Photostatic copies of this letter and attachment are being forwarded to the Anchorage Field Office. In accordance with request, suffices, dated July 20, 1947, Anchorage is requested to interview Captain JAKOB FINK and his co-pilot, VINTY [sic] COOK, presently at Beale AFB, in order to obtain all details of their reported sighting of a flying object on August 4, 1947. The San Francisco Field Office should be furnished two copies of the results of these interviews for distribution to the Sixth Army Intelligence Office.
