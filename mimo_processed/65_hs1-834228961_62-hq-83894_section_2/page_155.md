### RE. BUREAU:
Reference is made to Special Letter dated July 21, 1947, addressed to DR. A. S. HUBBARD, 66 Morris Park Avenue, Bronxville, New York, a copy of which was sent to the New York Office.

Mr. GARRABLE, was interviewed by Special Agent A. Johnson on August 12, 1947, at which time he advised that this coded message had originated in "Norway", the local newspaper of Massena and St. Lawrence County, New York. He stated that the message had appeared in this "County Incident", a feature of this paper, and had been signed "Radio Hat."

Mr. BON ARISTOLE, City Editor of "Norway", Roselle, New York, was interviewed and advised that this article had been sent to his newspaper by letter which was signed "Radio Hat", and explained that he had no way of determining the identity of the writer. Mr. ARISTOLE stated that it was his opinion that the code had been made up by some local "somebody" who had been affected by all of the newspaper publicity that had been given at that time to the story about flying discs.

He further noted an article appearing in the July 18th issue of "Norway" concerning this coded message, which might be of interest in this matter.

Unless advised to the contrary, no further effort is being made to establish the identity of the person who signed himself "Radio Hat".

Very truly yours,
