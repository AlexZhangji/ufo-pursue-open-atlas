## Office Memorandum — UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

TO: Director
FROM: SAC, Portland
DATE: September 3, 1947
SUBJECT: FLYING DISCS
SECURITY MATTER — X

***

Notes made to me before dated August 13, 1947 advise that a Mr. HOLLOWELL [?], of Salem, Oregon had reported on August 7, 1947 that he believed what he had observed to be a disc. Mr. HOLLOWELL stated that his news was witnessed by his son coming in one night while trying to notify a friend located across the air strip over the ocean. We agreed that he should be interviewed by special agent THURSTON of Portland. Mr. HOLLOWELL was a resident near Salem, Oregon and stated that his house was very near the ocean beach; that about 1:30 a.m. he was awakened by his son coming in one night while trying to notify a friend located across the air strip over the ocean. We agreed that he should be interviewed by special agent THURSTON of Portland. Mr. HOLLOWELL stated that his house was very near the ocean beach; that about 1:30 a.m. he was awakened by his son coming in one night while trying to notify a friend located across the air strip over the ocean. We agreed that he should be interviewed by special agent THURSTON of Portland.

Mr. HOLLOWELL went on to say that he had no idea as to the shape or size of this object but that it appeared to be about 15 feet across the ocean and that he was able to see it for about 15-20 seconds. He further stated that due to the distance he could not tell if wings were attached or not but stated that it was traveling a great deal faster than any airplane he had ever flown. He admitted that this glow of light was somewhat similar to "The Northern Lights" which sometimes appear in the sky but of course not nearly as large and that it appeared to be going up and down, that is, oscillating in its flight as it traveled along. He stated it disappeared in the west, rather falling into the ocean or disappearing over the horizon. He could give no other information concerning this object other than reported by him as possibly being a flying disc. Mr. HOLLOWELL has an 8th grade education about in Warfield in Wollard.
