July 31./52

To Federal Bureau of Investigation
The War Dept.
Washington D.C.

Gentlemen.: "FLYING SAUCERS"

Since reading & hearing over Radio about the "flying saucers",

I am compelled to write to You -

I am a strong believer in our almighty God & Christ our L. savior.

I have not bud.

I feel that these saucers are films from a secret Camera operated by something like T.V.

I do believe
