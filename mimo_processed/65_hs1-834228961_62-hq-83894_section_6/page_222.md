### Faint Mirror-Image Text (Bleed-Through from Recto)

*[Handwritten note, in mirror script at top left]:* A. L. Burks

*[Typed text in mirror script, header block]:*
FROM: J. Edgar Hoover
TO: Mr. H. B. Fletcher
SUBJECT: Dear Sir:

*[Typed text in mirror script, body paragraphs, transcribed verbatim as it appears (reversed)]:*
> I have been reading so much about the "flying saucers." Victor Scanner and I have spoken about it a number of times. We are the only two men at the station to witness such a phenomenon.
>
> Or could it be that these old objects are just that. And could be fitted or manufactured. It have been accepted by another station and no doubt they have received some information from to back up.
>
> I am not inclined to see those over here. Because I believe that these objects can not perform as shown to some extreme in some way in space.
>
> Also, I believe it is very unfair to make public the locations of all cities. As you now, there are a number of people around that exactly may make use of these places.
>
> And the one single thing that should not be done is the "Flying saucers" as it would also cause a lot of trouble. The military, could cause such a scare that the public could get out of hand.
>
> I hope that some day soon all of these objects are explained to the public. If they are found to be "saucers" then other countries should have already known.
> (INDEX)

*[Typed text in mirror script, closing block]:*
Attached you in U.S. Dept. of Justice
F B I
REC'D. ESPIONAGE
AUG 11 4 29 PM '53
