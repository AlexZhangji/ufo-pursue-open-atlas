1. The above-captioned subject was interviewed by Special Agent *[REDACTED]* and Special Agent *[REDACTED]* at 9:45 a.m. on 13 January 1958. Subject is a *[REDACTED]* in the United States Air Force and is presently assigned to *[REDACTED]* at this base.

2. Subject stated that on 13 January 1958 at approximately 8:20 p.m. while on his way to the Base Theater, he observed an object in the sky to the west of the base. The object appeared to be a disc-shaped object which was yellowish in color and appeared to be rotating. Subject stated that the object appeared to be stationary for approximately five minutes and then started moving slowly to the west, at which time Subject observed a second object of similar description. The second object appeared to be moving rapidly toward the first object, and the two objects merged into one. The combined object then appeared to move rapidly to the west and disappear. Subject stated that he did not observe any lights or other markings on the objects, and that he did not hear any sound.

3. Subject stated that he reported the sighting to the Base Security Police, who informed him that they had received numerous other reports of similar sightings.

4. This matter is being forwarded to the Director of Intelligence, USAF, in accordance with AF Regulation 200-2.
