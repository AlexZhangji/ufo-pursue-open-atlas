## Office Memorandum • UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

TO: DIRECTOR, FBI
FROM: SAC, WASHINGTON FIELD OFFICE-CO
SUBJECT: AERIAL OBSO (Flying Saucers)
DATE: July 28, 1952

On July 28, 1952, at the Weekly Intelligence Conference, Captain WILLIAM GORMAN, Office of Special Investigations, Fort Air Force Base, Tilling Field, advised the representative of the WFO that over the prior week, his office had received reports concerning the observation of aerial objects. Captain GORMAN referred to a phone call made to his office by Special Agent LILLIAN E. NEWHALL of the Washington Field Office, furnishing to OSI the identity of a woman who reported she had observed an aerial object. GORMAN stated he WFO for the assistance of Tilling in this instance.

Captain GORMAN, Technical Officer, and Special Agent JOHN M. DeBAUGH, in a conference on July 28, 1952, discussed the matter of immediate notification of OSI. The conference resulted in the request by Colonel GOULLIS and Captain GORMAN that in the event, whenever information is received by the FBI, in which it is alleged that someone has observed a "Flying Saucer", this information be immediately referred to Captain GORMAN's office. It is to be noted that this office may be reached by dialing Code 1281 and asking for Extension 509. Captain GORMAN said this number should be called under these circumstances at any time, day or night.

Captain GORMAN stated that the Air Force is greatly concerned about this matter of aerial objects and said that the Air Force Intelligence would appreciate the execution of the request mentioned above.
