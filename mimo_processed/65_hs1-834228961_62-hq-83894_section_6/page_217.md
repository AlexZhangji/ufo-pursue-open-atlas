## 4 Flying Saucers
### New York City
### July 31/52-
### Department of Investigations
### Washington D.C.

*[handwritten annotation:]* Benedicko Romero

### Dear sir:
Am sorry hearing and reading too; much noice about the mistery of them "Flying Plates"

*[handwritten note:]* 4

about some other things I talk in the same letter.
Ja !!! Ja !!! Ja !!!
I have to laugh because I know that the peaces comes very soon after I explain this mistery to my lovely government of the United States of America.
Nobody can explainth the mistery of the "Flying Plates" in a sincerely truth before me. Just make a try with the scientific people; and after call for me.

Sincerely with all my good will
Benedicto Romero
16 47 Park Ave. N.Y.
