EAST LOUISVILLE KY

MAY 1942

INFORMATION CONCERNING. THREE WOMEN SAW AIRCRAFT

OBJECTS FLOATING IN SKY OVER LOUISVILLE, KY. AT ABOUT 11 PM.

THEY NOTED GAVE IN SHAPE AND ACCORDING TO OBSERVERS COULD NOT BE

FOLLOWING TIME CLAUS OYSTERS WITH EXHIBITORS FLOODING LOUIS LAKE IN LOUIS.

THEY THINK THEY SAW FOR ONE TO THREE MINUTES. OBJECTS DESCRIBED AS

BEING LIGHT BLUE IN COLOR.

*[Stamp: BATTERSON - [Date/Time partially obscured] AM '52]*
