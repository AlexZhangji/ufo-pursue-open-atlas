## UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

**To:** Director, FBI
**Date:** August 6, 1952
**From:** SAC, Cincinnati (50-C)
**Subject:** ANOMALOUS COMMUNICATION RECEIVED IN ORDINARY MAILBOX RECEIVED BY "OSCILLANT ENGINEER" TREATING TO "FLYING SAUCER" SECURITY MATTER — X

Mr. K. DODGE of the staff of the "Cincinnati Enquirer" turned the Cincinnati Office with the enclosed letter and envelope received anonymously at the "Cincinnati Enquirer" in an envelope postmarked 7-3-52 at Cincinnati, Ohio.

It will be noted that the enclosed letter is written in German, and I am also enclosing a rather loose translation of the letter furnished by the "Cincinnati Enquirer".

In view of the widespread interest in so-called "flying saucers," I am forwarding this material to the Bureau, with the thought in mind that the Bureau may desire to have a careful translation made of the attached letter, and the results, if deemed advisable, made available to other Government agencies.

Enclosures (3)

FMK: jw
