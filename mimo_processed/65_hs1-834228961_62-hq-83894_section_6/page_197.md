*(Text is mirrored. Transcription follows the visible reversed letters and words.)*

```
UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

Mr. A. H. BROMLEY
Attorney F.B.I.
Federal Bureau

PURPOSE:
To advise at the present time the Air Force has
failed to correct any unauthenticated concerning the
frequent reporting numerous reports of flying saucer and
flying disk throughout the United States.

DETAILS:
Mr. H. P. FULTON, the Bureau's Air Force Liaison
Representative, made arrangements through the office of
Major General John A. Samford, Director of Air Intelligence,
U.S. Air Force to receive a briefing from General Samford
based on the current Intelligence Trends, Intelligence
Liaison, reporting the present status of Air Intelligence
Research into the numerous reports regarding flying saucers
and flying disk.

```
