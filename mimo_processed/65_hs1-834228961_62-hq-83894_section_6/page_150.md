RE FLYING SAUCERS, INFORMATION CONCERNING. THREE WOMEN SAW A STRANGE OBJECTS FLOATING IN SKY OVER ASHLAND, KY. AT EIGHT FIFTY PM, EST, MAY TWENTY FIVE LAST FOR TWO OR THREE MINUTES.
