##### SUBSIDIARY

The Minneapolis Division by letter dated 9/22/66, furnished the Los Angeles Division with Issue No. 24 dated July, 1966, entitled "FLIGHT SABOTAGE SUSPICIAL", which is the Official Journal of the Airdaft Flying Saucer Rader Club of America, Inc. The Minneapolis Division received the magazine from Mr. DAVID H. COOK, MRO, AIR, MO, North Powl Street, Minneapolis, Minnesota on 9/13/66. Mr. COOK stated he mailed the publication to the newspaper because his son had exhibited an interest in flying saucers and outer space. He said that on pages 2 and 3 of Issue No. 24 was an article which he followed the Communist Party (CP) line.

For the information of the Bureau, the Headquarters of their organization, the Airdaft Flying Saucer Rader Club of America, Inc., is located at 2004 North Hoover Street, Los Angeles, California. The article, on pages 2 and 3, were allegedly written by Master MAJOR-II PRAIM, Head of the Plant ROBOTCORP, which was relayed on 5/4/66 by special directional short-wave radio by BOB LAIN.

The Bureau of the Los Angeles Division control information identifiable with MAKED, and no investigation has ever been conducted on accepted organization.

No further action is contemplated in this matter by the Los Angeles Office, AOHO.

The foregoing is submitted to the Bureau for information.
