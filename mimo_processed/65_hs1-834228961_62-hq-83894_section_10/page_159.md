Enclosed for the Bureau are four copies of an LHM reflecting information provided by Sgt. PAUL STIGLIANO, United States Air Force, Recruiting Depot, Waco, Texas concerning the subject. One copy of LHM is also being furnished to Dallas.

This information is being furnished to the Bureau for proper dissemination to the military due to the particular request made by the subject concerning UFO (unidentified flying objects).
