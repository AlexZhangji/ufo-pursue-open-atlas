### Main Body Text (reversed text bleeding through from front)

> ...station at 4:30 p.m... Washington, D.C., based at Robins Air Force Base, Macon, Georgia, advised that at 2:30 p.m. he received orders directing that his office pick-up a high-altitude aircraft which would arrive at Robins Air Force Base at 5:30 p.m. He stated that the aircraft is a B-47 which is capable of flying at an altitude of 40,000 feet. He stated that he had been advised by the pilot... "There was no reason for the flight of the aircraft... The plane was in no danger and this flight would serve no useful purpose... There is a complete lack of communication and... This office has no information and there is nothing further to advise.
>
> The above information was furnished by Mr. John... of this office.
>
