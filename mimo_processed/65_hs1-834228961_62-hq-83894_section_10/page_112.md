### Memorandum

**TO:** DIRECTOR, FBI
**FROM:** SAC, DALLAS (62-1311)(C)
**SUBJECT:** UNIDENTIFIED FLYING OBJECTS - MISCELLANEOUS - INFORMATION CONCERNING
**DATE:** 1/23/68

>

There is enclosed for the Bureau one Xerox copy of a letter dated 11/20/67, from Mr. ROBERT G. EDWARDS, 145E Nottingham, Arlington, Texas, 76010, received by the Dallas Office on 1/19/68 in an envelope postmarked at Arlington, Texas, 1/17/68.

The enclosed letter reflects the information of a supplemental letter of the National Investigations Committee on Aerial Phenomena (NICAP) in the Dallas - Fort Worth area, the purpose of which organization is to conduct scientific investigation of unidentified flying objects.

This letter has been acknowledged and a copy of the same has been furnished OSI, Tinker AFB for its information.

The acknowledgment to Mr. EDWARDS pointed out to him that information concerning unidentified flying objects received by this Bureau is, by requirement, currently furnished to the U.S. Air Force.
