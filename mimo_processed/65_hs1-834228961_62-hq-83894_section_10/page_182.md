*Unidentified Flying Objects*

Mr. Stanley Schneider

Dear Mr. Schneider:

Pursuant to our conversation today, there is enclosed a copy of the FBI Law Enforcement Bulletin for the month of February, 1975. Beginning on page 16 there is an article covering the handling of UFO sightings which may be of interest to you in connection with your current inquiry.

If there is any way we may be of additional service to you, please do not hesitate to let me know.

Sincerely yours,

*[signed: Jay Cochran Jr.]*

Jay Cochran, Jr.
Assistant Director FBI
Technical Services Division
