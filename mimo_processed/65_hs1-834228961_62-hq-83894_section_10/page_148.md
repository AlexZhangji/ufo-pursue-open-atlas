**FLYING SAUCERS**

Dear Sir,

Just out of curiosity, if a person were to stumble across, build, or invent, a, shall we say, central drive unit that would power a flying saucer without the need of an exhaust or outward thrust that jets need, and I don't mean a fan or anything like that, But an engine like thing that could be placed in a completely sealed craft and would hang suspended in air or travel at speeds beyond mans comprehension, would it be considered top secret or anything? Also what should a person do with it?

An answer to this letter would be greatly appreciated by me.

Sincerely,
*[signed: Victor J. House]*

Victor J. House
P.O. Box 636
Montesano, Wash.
98563
