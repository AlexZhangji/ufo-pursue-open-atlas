## Incident: 182

A bright object similar in shape to the moon but with a distinct bright center was observed by the master of the S/S "Gulfcrest". The object gradually grew darker as daylight faded. Size was that of 1/4 of a full moon. It was traveling southeast by west.

First bearing: 2220 GMT - azimuth 350°
2d bearing: 2230 GMT - azimuth 358° 40.18
3d bearing: 2254 GMT - azimuth 050° 57.05

Disappeared at 2310 GMT.
