Incident: 209

Unidentified aircr ft observed at 1045 14 Nov 1948 over Army Observation Post #1 at 37° 57' N - 125° 31' E, in South Korea, flying east to west at an altitude of 5000 feet. The same aircr ft was sighted by army observation post #2 37° 51' N - 125° 17' E, 4. 37° 57' N - 125° 26' E and 5. 37° 56' N - 125° 29'
Subject aircraft circled observation post number 2, then headed north.
Aircr ft was twin engine bomber type, and is believed definitely to have been soviet.
