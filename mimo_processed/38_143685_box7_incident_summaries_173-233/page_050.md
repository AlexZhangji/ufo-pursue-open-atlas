## Incident: 195

On 31 Oct 1948 at 145 Z, GCA Operator at Goose Bay, Labrador noticed a target on PPI Scope Number 1. It (the object) appeared to be about 3 miles south of the field and was traveling on a southwest heading. It went into a ground clutter at 6 miles. The blip traveled at a speed of approximately 25 mph. This target sighting was verified by Base Commander C. D. Jones and by Group Commander Captain Verner, who were in the unit at the time.
