Incident: 184

Taken from Minneapolis Morning Tribune
21 October 1948

"LUCK! IT'S THOSE SAUCERS AGAIN"

... At Winona, Minn., Paul Zenk and Dr. F. T. Benoit said they saw a slowly traveling object which broke up into numerous bright particles over the town at 6 A. M. 20 Oct 48.

They said it was about 25 feet long and eight feet wide and appeared to be a "bunch of sparks with a tail."

NOTE The above witnesses were not contacted from Kold Chamberlain Fld, Minneapolis, Minn. See Incident 185 for report of L. A. Hansen and Harold Oliver.
