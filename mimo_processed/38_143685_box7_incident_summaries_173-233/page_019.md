## Narrative Account

Observer was walking thru the Golden Gate Park, when he observed a mysterious light that illuminated the entire ocean (insofar as he could see) He felt something "like an electric arc" which seemed to have the power to lower his hand "like a sack of shot". Observer states that he is subject to bruises and where the thing seemed to have contacted him he noticed a bruise the following day that had not been there before.

He stated that he couldn't find courage to report the incident since he didn't believe anyone would believe the story. He does not wish a further investigation.

**NOTE:** The account is very incoherent and the observer apparently not well educated. An extremely unreliable account.
