## Incident 223

At 2130 hours, 5 Dec 1948, Capt Goade, flying a C-47, No. AF 9921, enroute from Lowry AF Base, Denver, Colorado, to Williams AF Base, Chandler, Arizona, reported his position to the Control Tower, Kirtland AF Base, as being approximately 10 miles east of Albuquerque. Capt Goade stated he had seen a green flare arise from the ground to an altitude of approximately 500 ft on the east slope of the Sandia Mountains at about 2127 hours. He was flying at 18,000 ft. Stated he had just seen the same type flare at approximately 2105 hours just west of Las Vegas, New Mexico, at approximately 2105 hours.
