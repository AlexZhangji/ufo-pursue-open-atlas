### TSNAD-2B

**"Flying Saucers"**

Commanding General, Army Air Force, Washington 25, D.C.
ATTN: AC/AS-2, Lt Col Garrett

*[handwritten in margin]:* X 000.9 - Japan

It is requested that this office be furnished all available information concerning an alleged "Flying Saucer" flight sighting made by a radar station in Japan. This incident was mentioned by Dr. Charles Carroll during a conference in Brig Gen Schulgen's office attended by Mr. A.C. Loedding, T-2 representative, on 5 September 1947.

FOR THE COMMANDING GENERAL:
