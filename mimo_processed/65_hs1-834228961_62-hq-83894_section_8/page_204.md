## November 2, 1951

TO:
Dr. Howard P. Robertson
2022 Que Street, N.W.
Washington, D.C.

FROM:
General W. B. Smith

There is enclosed a copy of a letter of October 29, 1951 from the Secretary of the Air Force regarding the appointment of a Committee on Unidentified Aerial Objects.

It will be appreciated if you will agree to serve as Chairman of this Committee and obtain from the Secretary of the Air Force the necessary authorization and funding for the establishment of this Committee.

Will you please advise me by telephone as soon as possible?

## [Handwritten Annotation — reversed]

*[handwritten note, reversed]:* Dr. Robertson called me Nov. 6. He advised that he has talked with the Secretary of the Air Force and the Secretary of the Air Force has stated that he does not intend to appoint a committee on this subject.
