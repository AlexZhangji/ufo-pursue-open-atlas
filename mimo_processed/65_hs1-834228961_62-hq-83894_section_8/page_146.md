### MR. JONES, 21. LINDA M. WONG, LINDA L. MOORE N. KNECHT

Subject: S.C. Office, Intensive, employed as stenographer, attached to Domestic Intelligence Division, G.L. Jones, Court, made notations, placed at Washington Office.

OFFICIAL to advise M. & S. Office of Yellow Book reports have been submitted to G.L. Jones, Intensive, the A.I.D.

OFFICIAL made an annotated in A.I.D. Office, 10. LINDA MOORE, and made notations, "Yellow Book report, the 10. LINDA MOORE, and made notations."

WORLD because of one of these Yellow Book reports have been submitted to G.L. Jones, Intensive, made notations, "Yellow Book report, the 10. LINDA MOORE, and made notations." (O.B. - see "Notes and Office origin of the C.I. - M.F. Notes and Washington Office origin.)

A copy of this memorandum is being forwarded the Airborne Office for purpose of information.
