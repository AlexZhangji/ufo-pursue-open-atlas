### Details

Refers to the Bureau's letter of 9/21 and 7/22 re:

SUBJECTS: WILLIAM HAMPTON, PINE BLUFF, ARKANSAS; MICHIGAN STATE; TRANSPORTATION CONFIDENTIAL (104-2766); PIRATE 62-83894.

Attached for the Bureau's information is the 10/1/56
"Traveler" of the Civil Aeronautics Department affecting
subject; designated Vol. 1, No. 7, (9-17-56) and three other copies
of Vol. 6, No. 1 (9/10/56), (Enclosed herewith), and three additional
copies of Vol. 6, No. 10/17/56. The matter is furnished, in duplicate.
O.M.O., at the direction of the O.D. at this date.
A copy of this "Traveler" was furnished to SA W.J. CARTER on 11/1/56 for review by Col. E.F. LEOPOLD, DOW Air Force Base,
Brunswick, Maine. SA CARTER reported, in a call on 11/20, and after in
Liaison with Col. LEOPOLD, O.A.I. had been notified of the
accident and had sent investigators from Wright Air Force Base to
assist and make on the spot investigations in the case.

Enclosed is also enclosed a research report from A.C.
Investigative Unit (Maj. D/20/16). "RECONNAISSANCE", and three other
blue discs reports. This portion appears to be related to the "Traveler"
enclosed and also appears on the classified file at F/61/11 C.
UNIT. Col. LEOPOLD, O.A.R.C., D.O.W. Air Force, ALL DOD, D.D. Air
Intelligence, Wright Field.

In addition, those in reported in media clippings
cited being from the Dallas Times Herald of 10/17/56; 10/18/56;
