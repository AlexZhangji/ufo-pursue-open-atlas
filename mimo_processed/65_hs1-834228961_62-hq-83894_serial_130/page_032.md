### STATEMENT OF ALBERT SAMMS, CORDROY, NEWFOUNDLAND. TAKEN BY MERCEDES BURKE OF THE INTELLIGENCE OFFICE, HARMON FIELD NEWFOUNDLAND. 17 July 1947.

> Last Thursday night I was out by our house. I happened to look up and saw what I thought was an airoplane. It was flying at the height of a plane that is flying high. Then I thought it looked strange for an airoplane, because it was all lit up so bright, planes do have a light or two, but this thing was very bright - sort of a bright reddish yellow. I saw it only for a minute because it was travelling at a teriffic speed. It shot out of sight so quickly I thought it strange if it was an airoplane. *THEM*, I remembered what I'd been hearing about "FLYIND SAUCERS" I ran in and told my mother.
