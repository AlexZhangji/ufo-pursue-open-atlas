Due to the overcast conditions of the sky I was unable to see the aircraft until it had passed overhead and going away from me across a clear area where it was clearly visible for a period of about twenty (20) seconds. The aircraft was above the topmost layer of clouds or somewhere in the vicinity of 12,000 ft. (est.) altitude. At 07:47 A.M. a DC 4 aircraft passed over going in the same direction and on the same course at about 5,000 ft. altitude.

The peculiar differences are:

a - The craft was circular or possibly round in shape.

b - It was almost a silver white in color with a dark area at its center.

c - There was no trail of smoke or smoke as is usual with the jet.

d - Its speed was about 3 to 4 times greater than the above mentioned DC 4.

e - Its size, by comparison, would favorably correspond with a baseball held 30 ft. from the eye.
