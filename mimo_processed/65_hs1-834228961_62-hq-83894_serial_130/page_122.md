## Body of Letter
*[handwritten text:]*
On Sunday, July 20th, I observed an air-craft in flight that was unlike any I have seen to date and may shed some light on the "Flying Saucer Mystery".

I served four years - 1942 to 1946 - with the Army Air Forces as grade of Tech. Sgt. Signal Corps assigned to the Third, Eighth and Twentieth Air Forces respectively. During this time I had the opportunity to observe all types of aircraft and under all types of conditions but I never saw one like this one.

At 9:45 A.M. (D.S.T.) Sunday July 20th, I observed an airborne object that appeared to be circular or possibly round, light - almost white in color, with a dark area at or near its center, flying in a North West by West direction on the same routes used by Commercial Airlines between Washington, D.C. and Pittsburgh, Pa. The point of observation was approximately 22 miles East of Cumberland, Md. from the North bank of the Potomac River.
