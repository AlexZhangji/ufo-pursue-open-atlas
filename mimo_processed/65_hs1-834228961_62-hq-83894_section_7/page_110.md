## REVERSED TEXT (Reads left-to-right when mirrored; transcribed here in its apparent reversed state from the image)

DEPARTMENT OF CIVIL DEFENSE
JOHNSON COUNTY
FRANKLIN, INDIANA
NUMBER AND CADENCE STAFFS
28 January 1952

Major C.A. Hoover
Director, Dept. of Investigation
Indianapolis, Ind.

RENT SPACE:

I would first like to apologize for writing to you direct, however, I have explained this morning with our Civil Director and he gave me this procedure.

We have been having some nuisance in our Ground Observation Corps as we have four (4) posts here in Co. setup.

Last Fall I was contacted by one of our local businessmen wanting to know if I would be interested in joining the International Flying Saucer Bureau. I told him that I would join with the idea fully in mind of having no local people who are interested in flying because I do work in my Civil Defense Program. He said only too willing to cooperate in any way we can with Civil Defense. The closest Station is approximately thirty (30) miles southwest of Indianapolis, on the direct line between U.S. 31.

I am enclosing a letter which received last October, as well as our charter of flight hearing. Would like to know if this organization has been cleared or investigated in any way.

I would appreciate the courtesy if this information could be held so as not to scare away our source of information, if there is any.

If you do not approve of this please let me know.
