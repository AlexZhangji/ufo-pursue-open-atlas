## *Handwritten Letter*

*When I arrived home I spent approximately one half hour calling C.D. & Air Force Ground Operations etc. Finally after getting no answers I called the Waltham police who nicely informed me they didn't know what I should do about it. It came as rather a surprise to me. I thought that there was supposed to be somebody on lookout at all times. I do not know to whom I should write so in desperation am writing to you. I do hope you will report this to the proper authorities for me and I hope you will suggest to the local C.D. that they should have sometime or someone that a person could call to report such findings. I would appreciate very much a reply from you. May I just add that I honestly don't know what the unknown object was. I never before saw anything like it. I would be very happy to supply any other information you would like.*

*Thanking you very much*
*Sincerely yours*
*Mr. Robert H. Davison*

*[handwritten in margin: bottom left]*
*26 E. Scott St.*
*Watertown, Mass.*
