### SUBJECT: Comments on Article in the New York Times

> 1. A call was made to the Air Technical Intelligence Center, 1530 this date, (Captain Ruppelt) regarding the attached article.
>
> 2. ATIC did not in any way indicate to it. that the FBI has an interest in flying saucers. Furthermore, Captain Ruppelt stated that the FBI to his knowledge has never called upon to furnish reports on flying saucers. ATIC is under the impression that in, being made the story up or played up from a magazine or newspaper article is sometimes back that definitely was not associated with their organization.
>
> 3. ATIC suggests that you check further with Air Drop, Office of Public Information, and perhaps can supply you with the information you have requested.
