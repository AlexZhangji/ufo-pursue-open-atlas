## OFFICE MEMORANDUM • UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

### TO:
H. A. BORN

### FROM:
J. P. KAS

### SUBJECT:
FLYING SAUCERS

### SYNOPSES:
> Six Intelligence sources of another character are unexplained sightings of flying saucers. At Intelligence still feels flying saucers are optical illusions or domes of natural phenomenon but some lifting officials are seriously considering the possibility of interplanetary bases.

### CONCLUSIONS:
> You will recall that Air Intelligence has recently taken up with the National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics the matter of research regarding the so-called flying saucer problem. At Intelligence also problems now under study all research pertaining to this problem is being channeled through the Air Technical Intelligence Center located at Wright Patterson Air Force Base, Dayton, Ohio. Since then there has been a marked increase of the reported sightings of flying saucers and discussion of the implications and of exploratory objects such as radar balloons, etc., but that a small percentage of these unexplained sightings have been unexplainable.
