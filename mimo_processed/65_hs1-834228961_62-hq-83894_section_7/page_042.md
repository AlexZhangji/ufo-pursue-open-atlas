# OFFICE MEMORANDUM - UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT

To: A. H. CORD, OD
FROM: N. A. HEBER
SUBJECT: ATTENTION COPE

## PURPOSE:
To authorize transportation on military aircraft of persons connected with official Government business.

## BACKGROUND:
Captain William Dessart, USAF, requested authorization for Major Edward J. Ruppelt, USAF, and civilian employees of Project "Saucer" (Air Force, See.), to be temporarily attached immediately to my office; to be assigned with a flight of B-29's, and training for Project "Saucer." Captain Dessart states that the Air Force desires to fly personnel about the above-mentioned "Saucer," and that, if possible, it would be advantageous for the Bureau's cooperation in developing definite scientific knowledge concerning such phenomena.

## ACTION:
It is recommended that all or this memorandum be filed in the National Security and Supervisor's Room for our office for their information.
