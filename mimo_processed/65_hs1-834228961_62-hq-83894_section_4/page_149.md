## Hotel Lawrence Letterhead
**HOTEL LAWRENCE**
**Walnut Ridge, Ark.**

*[Handwritten in blue ink in the upper right corner]:* April 29th, 1949

## Recipient
*[Handwritten in blue ink]:*
Hon. J. Edgar Hoover:
Washington, D.C.

## Subject Line
*[Handwritten in blue ink, centered]:* "Flying Saucers"

## Letter Body
*[Handwritten in blue ink]:*
Having read the enclosed clipping you will find with this letter prompts me to send it to you and ask you to check The National Inventors Council files on the 2 types of engine & the Flying Saucer design that was submitted to Mrs. Eleanor Roosevelt in 1942, which Henry Wallace was Secretary of Commerce, and was in charge of The National Inventors Council.
I mean just this. See how easy it would have been for Wallace to have conveyed these designs to Russia and withheld them from our own Engineering Dept. You can make the biggest find here of your life, if you look I will appreciate a reply.

*[Handwritten signature at bottom]:* Cail L. Davis
*[Handwritten address below signature]:* 1410 Claire Dr. S.E.
Atlanta, Ga.
