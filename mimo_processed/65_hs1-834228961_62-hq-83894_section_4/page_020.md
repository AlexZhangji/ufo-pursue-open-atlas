## Salutation

Dear Mr. Omiline:

## Body

Your letter dated November 2, 1947, has been received. The information contained in the files of this Bureau has been declared confidential and available for official use only, and I am unable to be of any assistance to you. I am sure you will draw no inference from my inability to comply with your request.

## Signature Block

Sincerely yours,

John Edgar Hoover
Director

## Internal Note

NOTE: This individual asked questions concerning flying saucers and the Bureau's investigation of Russians.
