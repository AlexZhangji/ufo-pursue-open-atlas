## Covering Letter
*Handwritten letter on aged paper. A single hole punch or stain is visible near the top right corner.*

> Covington Ohio.
> Jan 29. 1948.
>
> F. B. I.
> Washington D. C.
>
> Dear Sirs:
>
> I have done a lot of thinking about those flying discs ever since your operators called at our home. I have figured out a possible solution that I am passing on to you. If you can see the picture as I see it.
>
> If self propelled bombs were made to travel 600 miles a few years ago, there is no earthly reason why they could not be made to go 3000 miles to-day. If a foreign country were planning to drop a lot of such bombs on our cities, they would want the exact range, and the only way they could get it would be to make some tests. I
