Now Mr. Hoover this was told me in 1944
or 43. At the time I wrote it all down
and never thought any more of it until
lately. And maybe I am foolish, but I
thought maybe this had something to do
with the flying saucers, if true.
I am a true American and love my
Country and if I can do any service
for my country I am glad of it.
The flying saucer could be more dangerous
than we think.

Respectfully yours.

Mrs. Frank Gardner

p.S.
I lived within a few miles of Mexican
border when this was told me.
In Texas.
