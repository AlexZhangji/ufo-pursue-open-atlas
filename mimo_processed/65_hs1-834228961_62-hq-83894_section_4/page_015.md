*[Handwritten text in blue ink on aged, slightly yellowed paper. Two dark circular holes punched at the top center, likely for filing.]*

### Sender (Top Right)
Mrs. Milo A. Durand
Elizabethtown
New York

### Salutation
Dear Sir:

### Letter Body
With all these forest fires in Maine and out west in our country I was wondering whether the flying discs had anything to do with it. Was that just a myth or did they exist. If so could not they have been incindary [sic] falling to earth then with drought and heat from the sun ignite. Were not the discs seen flying over Maine, Vt. and western states Are the western states that have forest fires the same ones reported to have seen the flying discs.

I have great respect for your bureau or should I say our. In many way the F.B.I. has been excellent. Visited the Bureau while in Washington some years ago.

I do hope I haven't taken up to much time but couldn't stop thinking of it. All Americans must be alert but not overly.

We have quite a lot of different foreigners settling up here with plenty of money. Like the back woods I guess. Keep to themselves too.
