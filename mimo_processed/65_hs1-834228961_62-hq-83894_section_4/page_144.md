EX 11

WITHHELD - (FO)

Reference is made to your letter dated April 5 and certain "confidential" information relative to visual installations. We are interested in whether or not further information relating to flying disks might be forthcoming.

It would be appreciated if you could advise whether or not further information on this matter can be made by Air Force Witness. This office has been advised by certain SA's, Air Defense, Army, Air Force, and Navy Intelligence, and other sources that they have no information, or knowledge of any such objects.

Our interview with Walter Kopp will be made by the Bureau obtaining the results of his experiences developed to its field offices.
