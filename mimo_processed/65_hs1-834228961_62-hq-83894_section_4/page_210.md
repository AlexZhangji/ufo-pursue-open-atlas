## MEMO BODY (REVERSED TEXT)

### Purpose

Your approval is requested in order that the attached letter and memorandum can be forwarded to the Phoenix Office, submitting, Mr. Lynn C. Adler, OSI Representative, Phoenix, Arizona.

### Background

In July, 1949, William Abbott Joyce was briefly interviewed by Special Agent D. Sallie B. Harrison (Bureau Agent) and George Paskay, Jr., A-2 Representative from Hamilton Field, California. The purpose of the interview was to secure from Joyce information and photographs of a flying disc, which had been signed and photographed by Joyce.

The complete results of the interview and subsequent events are contained in the attached memorandum submitted by the Phoenix Office.

Mr. Lynn C. Adler, OSI Representative at Phoenix, Arizona, has requested the Phoenix Office to furnish him complete notes relating to this interview, which are to be subsequently forwarded to the AF Intel Office at the Pentagon. According to Mr. Serge, related to above, has been questioned concerning this matter by his superiors and has advised them that the recollection is "hazy" on the matter. According to Mr. Adler, OSI has apparently now reported that Joyce has previously obtained from him.

### Recommendation

It is recommended that the Phoenix Office be advised that the memorandum attached may be forwarded by them to Mr. Adler, OSI Representative, Phoenix, Arizona.
