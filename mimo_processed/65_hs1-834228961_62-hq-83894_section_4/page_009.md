Mr. Hoover-
F.B.I. Flying Discs
Dear Sir.

They say that if one is crazy, one is the last to know it. That may be my case, but I hope not.

You know a about 2 months ago, a number of people said they saw flying discs, and the papers said it was imagination. I cannot believe that hundreds of people could imagine the same thing, at the same time, especially when separated by thousands of miles.
