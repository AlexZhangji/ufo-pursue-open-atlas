**'Saucer' Mystery Is Solved; Device Studies Weather**

One of the Washington area's flying saucers has been tracked down finally.

Last week a Martinsburg (W. Va.) woman found a mysterious five-foot square piece of aluminum covered material on her farm. She wanted to know what it was but nobody seemed to be able to tell and there was the usual speculation about flying saucers.

Andrews Air Force Base cleared up the mystery today. A spokesman said the object was used by the Air Weather Service. It is attached to a balloon and sent into the sky.

It is then tracked by radar and the direction and speed of the wind calculated. The device is in constant use, the air base said.
