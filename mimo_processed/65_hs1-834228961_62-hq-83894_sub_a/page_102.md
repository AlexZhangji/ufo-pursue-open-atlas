### Researchers' Balloons Mistaken for Discs
CHICAGO, July 16 (INS).
"Flying discs" and "fast-moving jet planes" that had some Chicagoans worried were identified today as gas balloons used by the University of Chicago for cosmic ray research. Large numbers of Chicagoans reported seeing mysterious objects in the sky yesterday morning. Descriptions varied from jet planes to silvery globules 40 to 50 feet long.
