## [Text of the document (visible from reverse side bleed-through)]:

### FBI. DEPT.
### Washington, D.C.

### Dear Sir:

I am extremely interested in the "Bender Affair", and I wish the most possible if you could help clear up the mystery in this affair. The reason I am writing you is because I have learned that a researcher of the flying saucer mystery has said the FBI is involved in this affair.

A civilian investigating agency was formed in Bridgeport, Conn. in 1952, to look into the flying saucer mystery by Albert K. Bender. He called the organization The International Flying Saucer Bureau. And, during his study of the saucer mystery Bender ran across something important. This information evidently led the mystery to a solution for in 1953 Bender stated bluntly, "I know what the saucers are." Then, "three men in black suits" influenced him. Even today Bender will not discuss the matter of his "hush-up" with anyone. However, I have learned that the secret of saucers lies the "druid underground mushroom" and that this secret is kept from the world. And, I am also extremely interested in this "saucer".

I have some very important information concerning this "druid underground mushroom" that I would like to discuss with Bender very much. For the story concerning this "secret" is certainly a "Mullin Horror" one, which reveals a fantastic situation, so fantastic, that it would be hardly believable to the public. Yes indeed, I would appreciate it very much if you would help me get in touch with Bender.

I have also been getting information regarding "certain" incidents that have occurred on the high seas for a long time, and "certain" aerial accidents and disappearances. For these tragedies reveal evidence that gives proof that such "secret" really exists.

I will certainly be looking forward in hearing from you.

*[Handwritten at bottom]:* U.S. Dept. of Justice
