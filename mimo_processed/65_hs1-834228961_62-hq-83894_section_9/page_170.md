*[Mirrored Text]: At this time there appears to be no necessity for additional instructions for the field or inspection in the FBI Handbook or Manual relative to flying saucers.*

*[Mirrored Text]: December 10-3-59 pointed out that our policy concerning unidentified flying objects is to conduct no investigation but immediately refer facts to Air Forces (see bulletin December 27, 10-1-47); then SAS Walter Harper stated, 5-25-49, restated this policy and set out our design for Air Force to be obtained when completed received; then SAS Walter, November 8, 5-29-5, again pointed out our policy; then SA. *[Mirrored Text]: During the past twelve months only one complaint regarding flying saucers has been referred for disposition. That immediately was turned over to Air Forces, both MS. and the SAC of Government.*
