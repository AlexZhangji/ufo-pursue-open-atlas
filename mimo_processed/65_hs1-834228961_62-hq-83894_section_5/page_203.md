## To the F.B.I.

*July 20/50*

### NR 8'11'50

Dear Sir:

The answer to your letter as near as I know.

This letter is to the best of my Remenry about the Beast Sauser that sit the top of a fig ash tree my truck park under It at night.

**FLYING SAUCERS**

When I came to the to Go to work I hurd a twistly Sound just as I was start to git in the trukle and then the Sauser sit the top of the tree and I taken of on foot left the truck and call Oak Park Ill Police at July 10/50 At 6:29 am oclock.

Then a four a month later I found a note in the trukle Seat when I was call out on a trip at 9:30 oclock at night I taken to the Police here.

*Or*

as near as I can Remenber it Said

Leauv the Cote that is a Peal of Paper that was in the Sauser Leave it out wher the Right Party can git.

On the Secon Paper it said the Sauser was not ment for you that is a Code of Male of big City an atomic Plant.

*Buisness dont mis.*
