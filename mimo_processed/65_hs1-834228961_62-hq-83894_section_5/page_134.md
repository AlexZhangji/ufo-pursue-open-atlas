# Federal Bureau of Investigation
Washington
D.C.

*Flying Saucers*

Gentlemen:-
I saw Ubalsty last Saturday in Willits, before he got on the bus to go to Eureka. I went to Willits for the mail for my son-in-law.
In our talk he said well if there is anything in the drawings of the mechanism of the saucer that will help you along on your cosmic ray engine use it - here are the drawings look them over and if you can find any thing to help you o.k. Those drawings are made in Russian I think so I could not read the instructions or lettering but I did see and he explained to me the centrifugal force that pulls the saucer - and the inside the saucer I seen the rudders of the ... just how they ...
