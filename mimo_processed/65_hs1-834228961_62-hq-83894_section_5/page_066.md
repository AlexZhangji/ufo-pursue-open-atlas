March 7, 1950

Mrs. Florence B. Pervier
1039 North College
Tulsa, Oklahoma

Dear Mrs. Pervier:
> Your letter of February 28, 1950, has been received and I want to thank you for making your observation available to me. Your thoughtfulness in this connection is appreciated.

Sincerely yours,

*J. Edgar Hoover*

John Edgar Hoover
Director

NOTE: Correspondent lengthily relates her observations concerning flying saucers and advised that she believes they are of Russia origin. It is noted that in her letter of February 14, she wrote to commend Mr. Hoover for his services as Director of the FBI.
