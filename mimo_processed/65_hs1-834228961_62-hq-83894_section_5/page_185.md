### Handwritten Letter Content

To whom it may concern:

I would like to know just how much truth is in Donald Keyhoe's book "The Flying Saucers Are Real" published this year by the Fawcett Publications, Inc. Keyhoe quotes a vast amount of information from the Air Force, and the F.B.I.

Sincerely yours,

J.L. Adams
