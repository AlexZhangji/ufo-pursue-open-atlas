## Page 3

Martin Aircraft Co. makers of the Martin Marauder. The craft is only made for one way trips. It has a range of 4000 miles. Ceiling of 25000 feet, and a speed of 450 miles per hour. So far only a few of these craft have been made, and they usually are ditched in the lake or ocean, as they cannot be landed. They are merely to carry a bomb of high destruction to enemy country. They have no wheels, but small steel rails on the bottom from which they take off. All other mechanism can be explained in detail.
