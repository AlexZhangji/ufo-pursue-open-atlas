### ADDRESSEE

Mr. L. A. Ward
336 Bird Street
Yuba City, California

### INDEXING AND FILE REFERENCE

INDEXED - 74
RECORDED - 74

### SALUTATION

Dear Mr. Ward:

### BODY

Your letters of April 8, 1950, have been received and I want to thank you for writing to me as you did.

It is suggested, however, that you may wish to communicate directly with the Secretary of the Army, National Defense Building, Washington, D. C., with reference to the matter you mentioned.

### CLOSING

Sincerely yours,

*[Signature stamped in purple ink]:* J. Edgar Hoover

John Edgar Hoover
Director

### INTERNAL NOTE

NOTE: Correspondent advises he has been in contact with the Buffalo Office and states that he has deemed the information he has furnished with reference to flying saucers important enough to write direct to the Bureau. He advises that he desires us to use a plain envelope in connection with our reply and further stipulates that he does not desire his name be furnished to "Army Chiefs." He has stated that he "wants to get a lot more information to give to you."
