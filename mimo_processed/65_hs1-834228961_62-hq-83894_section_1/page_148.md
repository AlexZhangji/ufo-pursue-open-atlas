## FEDERAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION
**Statement:**

Recently I have read and heard about reports of disc-shaped aircraft or saucer type disks, in our western regions. They remind me of a flying helicopter inclusive in Germany, after the war. I report this to you because I feel this may be of international scope.

My buddy and I went on Base to see a friend of his. On evening drive the three of us were driving along some bad roads when I sighted a strange looking object in the sky from旗 to tail wings to our front and side measuring 3,000 feet high.

I immediately stopped the jeep for a better look. The object rapidly came toward us, descending slowly. About a mile away it stopped its horizontal motion but continued a slow oscillating descent, similar to those descending helicopters. Then suddenly it dropped in a spiral motion.

Immediately I drove over to where it had dropped. It took almost five minutes to reach the place but we were rushing. About two minutes of circling around the area it became too dark to see so we went back to town.

I did not see any companions but I think it happened so quickly it could possibly have been launched, but I described what I had seen so vividly that they agreed as excited as I was. As I started in rotation that it was clearly a triangle of right angle formation.

The look of this incident was approximately 12 miles north-west of *[town name illegible]*. It fell west to the suburbality of this town and to the Base of Fort *[illegible]*.

*August 8, 1947.*
