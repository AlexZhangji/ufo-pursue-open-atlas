### DEPARTMENT OF THE AIR FORCE
**DATE:** 10 JUL 1947

### TO: DISTRICT DIRECTOR, INTELLIGENCE, 
### FROM: 
### SUBJECT: UNIDENTIFIED OBJECTS (Flying Discs) - REPORT OF

**Reference:** Attached are transcript copies from the Intelligence
Control and Dissemination Center (Report No. 204)

For the recorded information, dated 10th and 11th and received and disclosed to us at Los Angeles Air Force Office of Special Services (Intelligence) between 1:00 A.M. and 2:30 P.M., July 10th. The United States Representative at that time, Colonel W. H. Twining, Chief, Headquarters, Alaskan Department, stated that he had been in contact with the above mentioned group, which apparently was a special call from one of the highest possible publicized airplane in the country. The United States Representative decided not to make any statement to the press. I informed him that I had no official comments other than "No Comment."

Since I have contacted the United States Representative on various occasions, I asked him what the actual details were, and he stated he did not know exactly, but the information received indicated that a circular saw had been found with some device attached to it.
