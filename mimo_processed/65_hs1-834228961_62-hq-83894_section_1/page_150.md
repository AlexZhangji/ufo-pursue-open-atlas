STANDARD BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION
2483 S. MEMPHIS STREET
OKLAHOMA CITY 8, OKLAHOMA

TO: Chief, Air Materiel Command
ATTN: Air Technical Intelligence Center
WRIGHT-PATTERSON AIR FORCE BASE, OHIO

SUBJECT: FLYING DISCS

REFERENCE: Bureau File 62-HQ-9803

1.  Enclosed are two (2) photographs taken by *[REDACTED]*, 903 N. West 18th St., Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, on 29 July 1947.
2.  The photos were taken with a Kodak 35 mm camera, and show an object which the photographer states was approximately 20 feet in diameter and 6 feet thick. The object was observed for approximately 10 seconds. No sound was heard.
3.  The photographer states that the object appeared to be made of some type of metal, and that it was silver in color. The object was traveling at a high rate of speed, and was observed to make a sharp turn.
4.  It is requested that these photographs be given appropriate study and evaluation, and that this Bureau be advised of the results.
